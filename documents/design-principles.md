# VMSS Design Principles

The philosophical framework behind the doctrine. This document does not create law. It records the reasoning patterns that produced the law, so that future drafting, adversarial review, and Law Polling rulings stay on grain. When a proposed instrument conflicts with a principle here, the conflict is a signal to redesign the instrument — or, rarely, to amend this document through the same seriousness that produced it.

---

## 1. Consequence architecture, not permission architecture

Main Layer never prevents a lawful-but-foolish act. It prices the act, publishes the price, and lets the ledger collect. The door stays open; the burn is visible. Prevention is Sanctuary's instrument, purchased by its residents through demonstrated conduct and TIP consent. A prohibition proposed for Main is almost always a Sanctuary-shaped instrument in the wrong layer. Test: does the draft stop a citizen from doing something, or make the citizen pay for having done it? Main drafts do the second.

## 2. Structure over text

Textual prohibitions get reinterpreted, worked around, or ignored under pressure. Structural prohibitions either hold or visibly fail. Where a guarantee matters, build it into hardware, arithmetic, or architecture — the implant's privacy constraints, the SCM's pulse math, the novelty filter's shrinking jurisdiction. A promise that survives only as long as officials keep it is not a guarantee; it is a mood.

## 3. Two instruments, two functions

No instrument does another instrument's job. The tax collects revenue; the SCM prevents hoarding. The STI measures trust; the criminal record logs qualifying events. The implant observes; the assessment engine decides. When one instrument starts carrying a second function, either the second function gets its own instrument or the load is declared explicitly. Overloaded instruments are where capture begins.

## 4. Govern outcomes, never inputs

The civilization does not ask why a citizen owns, ingests, buys, or believes something. It holds the citizen fully accountable for everything that follows. Substance policy is the template: agnostic about the input, absolute about the consequence. Any draft that requires an adjudicator to classify a citizen's motive for non-criminal conduct fails this principle and should be rebuilt around the conduct's outputs.

## 5. The Flow Test

Capital that works is invisible to the anti-concentration envelope. Capital that warehouses value is savings, and savings circulate. Working capital evidences itself — settlement flow for commercial property, occupancy for residences, an active loan book for lenders. Idle capital self-identifies by silence. The test needs no inspectors because the evidence is generated by the activity itself or by its absence.

## 6. Attribution, not rate escalation

When stored value hides in a new denomination, do not raise rates on the old denomination — attribute the new one into the savings balance and let the existing pulse do the work. Rate escalation punishes whoever still holds the taxed form; attribution follows the value wherever it goes. Corollary: attribution triggers at the moment value becomes liquid or priced (loan origination, collateralization, barter realization), locking at that instant. Timing exploits die at the pulse-at-start principle.

## 7. Arithmetic before enforcement

The best consequence mechanisms are self-executing math: the SCM equilibrium, the overtime premium, the replenishment escalation, the currency retirement at layer crossings. A rule that needs an enforcement bureaucracy should first be redrafted to see whether arithmetic can deliver the same consequence. Bureaucracy is reserved for exploits that arithmetic demonstrably cannot reach — and the burden of demonstration is on the bureaucracy.

## 8. No bureaucracy without a live exploit

Institutional machinery is added when a real exploit needs a heavy hand — never for symmetry, tidiness, or the comfort of coverage. An exploit that loses money for its user, extinguishes itself under crowding, or converts stock into circulation is not an exploit. It is a donor, and the correct ruling is to do nothing. "Do nothing" is a complete and honorable answer in this framework, distinguishable from negligence by the designer's ability to state why the nothing is there.

## 9. Accepted tension is a category, not a failure

Some pressures are priced in rather than patched: parental manipulation against the child's exit right, lower-layer counter-culture, cash-analog leakage in the lower economies, conspicuous waste in Main. Declaring a tension accepted requires two things — a stated reason the available fixes cost more than the tension, and a tripwire: the observable pattern that, if it appears, reopens the file. Deferral with a tripwire is doctrine. Deferral without one is hope.

## 10. The redundant envelope

No load-bearing function depends on a single instrument. Surveillance, offense, continuity, anti-concentration, and governance integrity each run three to five stacked layers so that evasion of any one routes into the next. When drafting a new load-bearing function, the envelope is the design target — the question is never "does this instrument hold" but "what catches the actor who evades it."

## 11. Units of account are design choices

The framework's deepest moves changed what a quantity is denominated in. Consequence: denominated in place, not time — which is why sentences scale to any lifespan. Wealth: denominated in flow, not stock — which is why dynasty has no substrate. Legitimacy: denominated in the border queue, not the ballot. Status: denominated in ledger record, not narrative. Before importing any Earth mechanism, check whether the quantity it manipulates still exists in VMSS denominations. Most Earth attacks and most Earth fixes dissolve at this step.

## 12. Layer character is load-bearing

Every instrument carries an enforcement posture, and the posture must match the layer. Sanctuary: institutionally heavy-handed, safeguarded, preventive — the 1+1 property rule lives here on purpose. Main: free-spirited, consequence-priced, open-doored — attribution and the pulse live here. Lower layers: withdrawn, organic, private — VMSS does not reach into what it renounced. A rule migrating between layers must change shape to match the destination's character or it corrupts the layer it enters.

## 13. Hidden-parameter audits

Every mechanism silently assumes some environmental constant. Sentences assumed lifespan. Post-intervention assumed harm reversibility. UBI figures assume a value peg. The audit question for any core mechanism: what constant does this quietly depend on, and which planned technology or doctrine changes that constant? Mechanisms with no hidden parameter are load-bearing walls. Mechanisms with one are where the next century's LPs come from.

## 14. Maintenance and structure never share an author

Calibration within a fixed category is operational maintenance; changing the category set is structural amendment. The STI weights, Meritboard calibration, SCM settlement rates, and district redraws are maintenance. The dimension sets, metric categories, trigger architecture, and layer criteria are structure, reachable only through the appropriate gauntlet. No entity ranked by a metric designs that metric. No system self-certifies.

## 15. The gauntlet prices change; it does not forbid it

Nothing is textually cemented. Every tier can be rewritten at a cost calibrated to its structural weight, and the founding core is protected by expense rather than prohibition — only a civilization genuinely beyond it will pay the price, and at that point the cost is legitimate. Drafting implication: never propose an unamendable clause. Propose the correct tier.
