# LP-075 §13.1 record-gap notice

**Repository audit notice — not a backdated amendment record.**

The repository was searched for the cold adversarial review required before
the 2291 adoption vote on LP-075. No artifact establishes all of the following:

- the name and eligibility of the mechanically selected reviewer;
- a review completion date before the adoption vote;
- the review findings;
- drafter dispositions;
- the reviewer's written reply to each disposition;
- unresolved standing objections;
- presidential flagging of any highest-severity finding adopted over objection.

The existing Charter and Schedule cold-review materials concern the 2279
methodology and its §10.4 Schedule. The `first-run-*` materials concern an
illustrative 2279–2281 chronicle. Neither is a pre-enactment LP-075 amendment
review under Charter §13.1.

LP-075 remains recorded in the law register as enacted procedural law. This
notice does not silently repeal or rewrite it. It records the narrower
authority-chain conclusion: until a contemporaneous qualifying artifact is
identified, the repository cannot use LP-075's asserted amendment compliance
as independent proof that the purported 2292–2294 remedial run was valid.
