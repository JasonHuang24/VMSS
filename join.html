Ready to Join The Five Rings?

The choice — and the consequences — are yours.

What to Expect

  • • Voluntary entry — implants are opt-in; no coercion
  • • Full dignity from day one — $10,000/month UBI in Main Layer (0)
  • • Freedom + consequence — create, love, explore; harm = permanent layer reassignment
  • • Backup vessels — continuity insured (0.5–2% drift risk)
  • • Ascension path — higher-trust layers await the compliant
  • • Pre-intervention in +1 Sanctuary — acts halted mid-execution
  • • Post-intervention in Main & below — acts may complete; victims revived/treated
  • • Five Rings structure — concentric mega-walls, vertical transit

Next Steps

  1. Review the Charter and Layers
  2. Understand the risks (permanent layer reassignment, STI visibility)
  3. Consent to implant procedure (voluntary)
  4. Begin your life in Main Layer (0)
  5. Ascend through compliance and contribution
  6. Opt into Selective Ascension Domains as you qualify

Who Should Join

  • • People who want a civilization where trust, safety, and opportunity are tightly linked
  • • People willing to trade ambiguity for clear consequence
  • • People who want longer life, biological freedom, backup continuity, and technological abundance
  • • People who believe social harms should carry real consequences even when they are not criminal

What Joining Actually Means

Joining The Five Rings is not simply moving to a better city. It is opting into a different civilizational contract.

  • • You begin in Main Layer (0), not in Sanctuary.
  • • You receive dignity, UBI, safety infrastructure, and access to advanced technologies from day one.
  • • Your outward actions become legible to a system built around consequence, STI accountability, and layer reassignment.
  • • You remain fully free to act — but harmful acts permanently change the environment you are allowed to inhabit.

In short: VMSS offers far more than Earth, but it also asks far more of your behavior.

Before You Apply

  • • Read the Layers page so you understand what is at stake
  • • Read the FAQ so you understand permanence, continuity, STI, and enforcement
  • • Do not join if you want the benefits without accepting the consequences
