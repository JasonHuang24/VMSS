Economy & Work

No. The Primary Job Subsidy qualifies work based on contribution to critical infrastructure — systems the civilization needs staffed to function. It is a labour allocation mechanism, not a moral endorsement system. A citizen earning millions as a performer, artist, or entrepreneur is doing something genuinely valuable. The subsidy was never designed for them and becomes trivial at that income level. A citizen doing grey-market consulting at $50 per hour without qualifying is neither prohibited nor stigmatised. Three categories coexist freely: qualifying work that earns the subsidy, non-qualifying ethical work rewarded by the market, and grey-area work that is legal but not subsidised. The civilization has no objection to any of them.

A Sanctuary resident cannot transfer their wealth downward, and wealth accumulated in a lower layer cannot be converted upward. Yes. Voluntary descent triggers progressive liquidation rather than full confiscation. Citizens retain between 1% and 10% of liquidated assets depending on net worth at time of filing, converted to destination layer currency. The schedule runs from 90% treasury / 10% retained at under $1 million, scaling to 99% treasury / 1% retained at over $1 billion. Voluntary descent is a legitimate civilizational choice and must remain economically viable — the system captures most of the value while preserving enough to establish yourself in the new environment. Your upward pathway also remains open: voluntary descent does not permanently close ascension eligibility.

The retained capital lands in an environment where the voluntary resident's non-financial assets are worth considerably more than what was liquidated. Upper layer education and skills are rare in -3's frontier economy — technical expertise that is commonplace in Main Layer commands a significant premium where automation infrastructure is thin and human labour fills the gaps. A clean voluntary descent record on the public ledger signals legitimate choice rather than punitive reassignment, granting day-one access to gated communities that exclude the punitive demographic entirely. Skills, status, and network compound the seed capital rapidly. The percentage looks small. The asymmetric advantage it arrives with does not.

A savings circulation mandate applies across all layers, scaled to each layer's institutional presence and UBI baseline. The thresholds and rates differ but the corrective logic is the same at every tier.

+1 Sanctuary and Main Layer: When population-average savings combined crosses $100,000 per citizen, a 10% monthly garnishing activates uniformly — no floor, no exemptions. At $10,000/month UBI the equilibrium sits at exactly $100k. A whale holding $5 million loses $500,000/month against $10,000/month UBI replacement. Their savings collapse unless deployed into productive activity. The mechanism doesn't harm average citizens — it makes idle capital at scale economically irrational.

-1 Noncompliance: Same logic at half scale. Trigger is $50,000 average per citizen, rate is 5% monthly. At $5,000/month UBI the equilibrium sits at $50k. Proportionally equivalent pressure on whales.

-2 and -3: VMSS doesn't touch private gains in punitive layers. The mandate applies only to savings attributable to VMSS-distributed funds — UBI and subsidy payments. Triggers are $25 million aggregate in -2 and $10 million aggregate in -3, at 5% monthly on the UBI-origin portion only. Attribution uses a 24-month rolling window comparing cumulative UBI receipts against current savings — no implant required, a bank statement suffices. Private earnings from those layers' organic economies are entirely untaxed.

All garnished funds at every tier return to the Automation Dividend Treasury and redistribute as UBI. The cycle is self-terminating — it deactivates when the aggregate drops below threshold. Idle capital becomes everyone's civilizational dividend.

Visitation & Cross-Layer Movement

They are fundamentally different acts. Visitation is temporary. You retain your origin layer status, your origin layer assets, and your full institutional relationship with the civilization. No liquidation occurs. You arrive economically neutral in the destination layer — your origin currency doesn't spend there — and you participate in the local economy by earning local currency through work or receiving it from residents. Voluntary descent is permanent change of status. You file formally, your assets are liquidated per the progressive schedule, your layer placement changes, and your institutional relationship is restructured. Visitors can leave whenever they choose. Voluntary residents have changed their address in a meaningful sense.

The same reasons people visit places that offer something genuinely different. -3 is not a degraded version of Sanctuary — it is a distinct environment with its own economic texture, social character, and freedoms that Sanctuary cannot replicate. The rawness of operating without institutional backing, the frontier economy, the authenticity of an environment where nothing is pre-managed — these have genuine appeal for certain kinds of people. A Sanctuary visitor arrives economically neutral regardless of their upper-layer wealth. They have to work a job in -3 or know someone there to participate economically. That levelling is part of what makes it interesting rather than just a disaster tourism exercise. They cannot exploit the tax differential because their currency doesn't convert.
