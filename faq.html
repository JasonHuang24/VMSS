No liquidation occurs. Voluntary descent is permanent change of status. You file formally, your assets are liquidated per the progressive schedule, your layer placement changes, and your institutional relationship is restructured. Visitors can leave whenever they choose. Voluntary residents have changed their address in a meaningful sense.

The Meritboard certifies competence for the highest roles and serves as the talent pool from which the President, the presidential deputy, and Supreme Court justices are drawn. The Supreme Court of 10 justices handles edge cases where automated governance and existing doctrine produce ambiguous outcomes. No elections, no constituencies, no campaigns. Competence is measured, not voted on. Full governance structure in the Charter.
