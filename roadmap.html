Development Roadmap

From the 2026 Boise Accord to a mature civilization at 0.01% leakage by 3000

This roadmap represents a staged evolution of VMSS from conceptual foundation to large-scale civilization deployment. Each phase prioritizes validation, stability, and controlled expansion before progression. Nine phases across 974 years — from a signed accord in Boise to a civilization approaching the theoretical limits of consequence delivery.

0

Phase 0: Concept & Legal Foundation (2025 – Mid 2026)

The foundational year where the idea becomes reality.

  • Boise Accord signed in Idaho on January 26, 2026 — the legal and philosophical birth certificate of VMSS.
  • Creation of a sovereign charter entity and non-profit foundation to hold land and technology rights.
  • Core team assembled (engineers, neuroscientists, ethicists, lawyers, and visionary funders).
  • Seed funding secured ($1–3 billion) from aligned high-net-worth individuals and forward-thinking funds.
  • First public whitepaper and ethical framework released to the world.
  • Initial governance stress-tests conducted in simulated environments to identify structural contradictions before deployment.
  • Baseline ethical constraints, amendment boundaries, and oversight assumptions formalized for future scaling.

Population: 0 → ~150 (core team + early volunteers)

1

Phase 1: Core Technology Prototyping (2026 – 2032)

Building the nervous system of the new civilization.

  • First functional human-grade implant prototypes tested in animals, then limited human trials.
  • STI ledger and real-time intent-detection algorithms proven reliable.
  • Backup vessel animal revival success achieved (target <2% drift risk).
  • Graphene wall material and autonomous drone swarm prototypes tested in controlled environments.
  • Early neural diving demos (Audience Mode) successfully conducted.
  • System stress-testing performed under simulated adversarial conditions to identify manipulation vectors and failure modes.
  • Development begins on multi-factor evaluation models beyond STI to support non-deterministic classification.

Population: ~150 → 1,000 (closed team + early volunteers)

2

Phase 2: First Enclosed Habitat & Proof-of-Concept Ring (2032 – 2038)

The first physical ring where the full system is tested with real citizens.

  • Construction of the first small prototype ring (10–20 km diameter) in a remote location.
  • Full UBI + Primary Job Subsidy system launched and tested.
  • Pre-intervention enforcement successfully demonstrated in a small Heaven zone.
  • First 5,000–25,000 voluntary citizens admitted under strict screening.
  • Public data on moral causality and revival reliability released to build global trust.
  • Behavioral analysis under real participant conditions evaluates divergence between laboratory models and lived outcomes.
  • Oversight mechanisms are introduced to monitor system drift, trust distortions, and unintended incentives.

Population: 25,000

3

Phase 3: First Full Ring & Multi-Layer Validation (2038 – 2050)

Scaling from prototype to functional civilization.

  • Completion of the first full Main Ring (50+ km diameter).
  • Construction and activation of -1 and -2 layers.
  • Reliable human backup vessel revival achieved (<1% drift).
  • First Heaven Layer ( +1 Sanctuary) operational with initial SADs.
  • Earth migration program opens to carefully screened applicants.
  • Cross-layer feedback systems are studied to understand long-term behavioral adaptation under different incentive structures.
  • Transition rules are refined to reduce volatility while preserving accountability, legibility, and system trust.

Population: 100,000 → 5 million

4

Phase 4: Full Five-Ring Civilization (2050 – 2070)

The complete concentric structure becomes reality.

  • All five concentric rings completed and fully operational.
  • +1 Sanctuary fully functional with dozens of active SADs.
  • -3 Terminal active as a zero-intervention environment.
  • Inter-ring transit optimized with high-speed vertical maglev shafts.
  • Lifespan extension reaches 200–250 years for the majority of residents.
  • Layer distribution is stabilized across large populations to prevent concentration collapse, stagnation, or artificial bottlenecks.
  • Governance, economic, and enforcement incentives are aligned to keep the system coherent under planetary-scale load.

Population: 10 million → 200+ million

5

Phase 5: Planetary Consolidation (2070 – 2150)

The full five-ring civilization reaches planetary scale and begins confronting the realities of long-duration operation.

  • Full planetary population across all rings reaches billions. Layer distribution stabilises across large populations.
  • Orbital habitats and first permanent off-world outposts established using VMSS principles.
  • Lifespan extension reaches 250–300+ years practical for most citizens.
  • Wall construction phases underway — prototype sections of the full 15km-above / 5km-below / 100m-thick boundary infrastructure completed and tested.
  • VMSS becomes the dominant governance model for new human settlements beyond Earth.
  • Early interstellar probe missions launched with backup vessel technology aboard.
  • System leakage estimated at approximately 25% — formative infrastructure operational but incomplete. First public leakage report issued. The 0.01% aspiration formally adopted as the civilizational standard.
  • Edge cases from 80+ years of operation systematically catalogued and addressed through charter amendment process.

Population: 1–5+ billion across the rings and beyond

6

Phase 6: Infrastructure Hardening (2150 – 2350)

Two centuries of systematic construction and leakage reduction. The civilization transitions from prototype to proven architecture.

  • Full wall network construction across all five ring boundaries — the largest continuous engineering project in human history.
  • Sub-surface 5km wall foundations proven to eliminate tunnelling approaches. Above-stratosphere 15km height confirmed impervious to civilian aviation.
  • Backup vessel technology matures — drift risk declining from 2% toward sub-0.5% through successive generations of reconstruction science.
  • Enforcement drone coverage expands toward full saturation within Main Layer and above.
  • AI governance systems accumulate two centuries of edge case data — classification accuracy reaches new benchmarks.
  • The lower layer private economies mature. -3 sub-stratification into voluntary and punitive communities is well-established. Private justice infrastructure operates reliably across punitive layers.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~25% (2150) → ~15% (2250) → ~8% (2350).
  • First Dyson swarm feasibility studies completed. Energy planning horizon set at 2600 for first partial deployment.

Leakage target by end of phase: ~8%

7

Phase 7: Systemic Refinement (2350 – 2600)

The civilization reaches operational maturity. Leakage enters single-digit territory. The focus shifts from building to refining.

  • Full wall network complete and operational across all layer boundaries. Boundary breach now requires extraordinary organised effort detectable by seismic and sensor networks before completion.
  • Backup vessel drift approaches near-zero through mature reconstruction biology. Continuity guarantee becomes reliable at civilizational scale.
  • Enforcement network achieves near-complete coverage — apprehension failure becomes the exception rather than the rule.
  • First partial Dyson swarm segments operational by 2600, beginning the transition to energy abundance beyond planetary renewable capacity.
  • Charter amendments through this period reflect 350+ years of lived civilizational experience — the document matures from founding framework to refined operating constitution.
  • Cross-layer cultural identity is well-established. The informal layer names — the Freedom Layer, the Balanced Layer, the Lower Restrictions Layer — are common civilizational vocabulary.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~8% (2350) → ~4% (2450) → ~2% (2550) → ~1% (2600).

Leakage target by end of phase: ~1%

8

Phase 8: The Forcefield Century (2600 – 2850)

Dyson-class energy enables a new class of boundary infrastructure. The civilization prepares for its final approach to mature operation.

  • Dyson swarm energy output reaches levels sufficient for sustained planetary forcefield maintenance — the threshold that makes the forcefield upgrade viable shifts from theoretical to engineering.
  • Forcefield prototype tested on a single wall section by 2750. Results validate integration architecture.
  • Partial forcefield integration across primary layer boundaries by 2800. Wall breach leakage drops sharply — the category that was previously the largest single leakage source becomes functionally negligible.
  • Full forcefield network operational by 2850. Physical mega-wall and energy barrier now operate simultaneously — the boundary infrastructure is layered, redundant, and impenetrable by any means available to individuals or organised groups.
  • AI governance maturity reaches levels where edge case classification accuracy approaches theoretical limits. The system has now processed millennia-equivalent decision data through simulation and centuries of real operation.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~1% (2650) → ~0.5% (2750) → ~0.3% (2800) → ~0.1% (2850). Parabolic acceleration begins.

Leakage target by end of phase: ~0.1% — the parabolic acceleration commences

9

Phase 9: Mature VMSS (2850 – 3000)

The final approach. Technologies converge. Leakage collapses parabolically. The civilization the founding accord envisioned becomes real.

  • Dyson-class energy abundance enables enforcement network densities previously impossible — coverage gaps that produced apprehension failures essentially disappear when energy constraints are lifted.
  • Backup vessel drift eliminated as a meaningful failure category. Reconstruction biology has reached theoretical maturity — the continuity guarantee is as close to absolute as physics permits.
  • Forcefield and physical wall operating in complete integration. The boundary infrastructure of the civilization has no known bypass mechanism available to any non-civilizational actor.
  • AI governance systems operating at maximum achievable accuracy after nearly a millennium of refinement. The judgment calls that produced failures in formative phases are now handled with precision that would have seemed impossible to the founding generation.
  • The four major technology convergences — forcefield, energy abundance, backup vessel perfection, AI maturity — arriving within the same 150-year window compound each other's effect. This is the parabolic spike. The last 0.09% of leakage is eliminated faster than the preceding 24.91%.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~0.1% (2850) → ~0.05% (2900) → ~0.02% (2950) → ~0.01% (3000).
  • The civilization of 3000 measures its consequence delivery at 0.01% leakage across all categories. What the founding accord promised in 2026, the mature civilization delivers in 3000 — not perfectly, but as close to perfectly as any human institution has ever come.

Leakage target: ~0.01% — mature VMSS achieved

From a single signed accord in Boise, Idaho in 2026 to a civilization operating at 0.01% leakage by the year 3000 — this is not fantasy.
This is a 974-year project built on moral causality, advanced technology, and the conviction that consequence and consequence delivery can approach perfection together.
