Development Roadmap

From the 2026 Boise Accord to a mature civilization at 0.01% leakage by 3000

This roadmap represents a staged evolution of VMSS from conceptual foundation to large-scale civilization deployment. Each phase prioritizes validation, stability, and controlled expansion before progression. Seven phases across 974 years — from a signed accord in Boise to a civilization approaching the theoretical limits of consequence delivery. The starting leakage is approximately 90%. The target is 0.01%.

21st Century — Where We Actually Are

The Boise Accord establishes VMSS as a civilizational target. It is not a construction schedule. Using a weighted assessment across all technology categories, VMSS today delivers approximately 10% of its stated promise — or equivalently, operates at approximately 90% leakage across all enforcement and infrastructure categories combined. Leakage is the gap between stated consequence and actual consequence delivery: every wall breach, apprehension failure, backup vessel death, or enforcement network gap is leakage. The founding aspiration of VMSS is 0% leakage. The 974-year roadmap is the story of closing the gap from 90% to 0.01%.

The leakage figure is not uniform across categories. Three are load-bearing — backup vessels, the implant ledger, and autonomous enforcement. These have veto weight: the civilization's core promises fail without them regardless of progress elsewhere. The overall figure is pulled toward 90% primarily by these three gaps, not by the average of all categories.

Load-Bearing Gaps
  • Backup vessels — 0% delivery
    No functional analog exists. Cryonics has no successful revival record. The continuity guarantee is a complete miss with current technology — the single largest leakage category by weight.
  • Implant ledger — ~10% delivery
    Security cameras, forensics, and behavioral scoring infrastructure exist but are fragmented, gameable, and lack continuous identity-anchored monitoring. China's social credit system proves national-scale behavioral scoring is feasible. The gap is comprehensiveness and the identity anchor.
  • Autonomous enforcement — ~5% delivery
    Drone hardware prototypes exist militarily. The contextual judgment layer required for VMSS enforcement doesn't exist at civilian scale. Police response times average 10–15 minutes. The US-Mexico border wall — the closest physical analog to the megawall — demonstrates what boundary infrastructure looks like without full sensor and response integration.
Partial Foundations
  • UBI / economic model — ~20% delivery
    Finland, Kenya, and Stockton pilots prove feasibility at small scale. Alaska's Permanent Fund has distributed automation-equivalent dividends since 1982. The economic model is proven in principle. The political will and funding mechanism at civilizational scale are not.
  • Biological augmentation — ~25% delivery
    Cosmetic surgery, gene editing (CRISPR), organ transplantation, and joint replacement prove the concept. Leg lengthening surgery is a crude analog for body scaling. The direction is established and advancing rapidly.
  • Voluntary entry infrastructure — ~40% delivery
    Citizenship by naturalisation, intentional communities, and religious orders prove humans will voluntarily opt into rule-governed civilizational contracts with defined consequences. The concept is mature. The scale is not.
  • Pre-intervention / TIP — ~2% delivery
    Restraining orders are the closest analog. Essentially non-functional as a VMSS equivalent. This category requires backup vessel technology and implant ledger to exist first.

The conceptual and institutional foundations are further along than the headline leakage figure suggests. VMSS is not waiting for someone to have the ideas — the ideas exist in primitive form across existing institutions. It is waiting for enabling technologies to make those ideas operable at civilizational scale with the precision the framework requires. Phases 0 and 1 are not construction. They are the period in which those technologies must cross their critical thresholds before construction becomes possible. We are drafting blueprints for an aircraft in an era without aluminium, combustion engines, or aerodynamic theory. The blueprint is sound. The world needs time.

0

Phase 0: Foundation & Research (2026 – 2070)

The civilizational blueprint is established. The enabling technologies do not yet exist — this phase is the period in which they must begin to be developed before construction becomes possible.

  • Boise Accord signed January 26, 2026 — the legal and philosophical birth certificate of VMSS. Sovereign charter entity and non-profit foundation established to hold land and technology rights.
  • Core team assembled across neuroscience, materials engineering, AI systems, ethics, law, and governance. Seed funding secured from aligned long-horizon investors.
  • First public whitepaper and charter released. Governance stress-tests conducted in simulation to identify structural contradictions before any physical deployment.
  • Brain-computer interface research intensifies — early implant prototypes tested in animal models, then limited consenting human trials. No timeline for civilian deployment; this is foundational science, not product development.
  • Backup vessel research initiated as a long-horizon programme. Early work focuses on mind-state encoding theory and synthetic biology for vessel growth. Animal-scale continuity experiments begin. Human-grade revival remains decades away.
  • STI ledger architecture designed and stress-tested in closed simulation environments. Behavioral scoring algorithms developed and audited for manipulation vectors.
  • Autonomous drone enforcement prototypes tested in controlled settings. Materials research begins on advanced composite wall construction at scale.
  • UBI pilot programmes expanded and studied globally. Economic model validation continues at increasing scale — proving the automation dividend concept before civilizational deployment.
  • No physical ring construction in this phase. The 21st century ends with the framework established, the research underway, and the first technologies beginning to approach — but not yet crossing — their critical thresholds.

Population: Core research team + volunteers. Leakage: ~90% → technology thresholds not yet crossed.

1

Phase 1: Technology Threshold Crossing & First Prototype (2070 – 2150)

The load-bearing technologies begin crossing their critical thresholds. For the first time, meaningful physical infrastructure becomes possible.

  • Backup vessel technology achieves first reliable human revival in controlled conditions — the single most significant technological event in VMSS history. Drift risk still high (5–15%) but the category crosses from zero to functional.
  • Neural implant ledger achieves continuous identity-anchored monitoring at small scale. STI computation proven reliable in a closed community environment.
  • Autonomous enforcement reaches civilian deployment threshold — contextual judgment layer operational, response times approaching seconds rather than minutes in prototype zones.
  • First small enclosed prototype community established — hundreds to low thousands of voluntary citizens operating under full VMSS conditions. Not a ring, not a mega-wall — a controlled settlement proving the integrated system works before physical scaling begins.
  • Full UBI and Primary Job Subsidy system operational within the prototype community. Economic model validated under real conditions with real citizens.
  • First sections of boundary wall infrastructure designed and initial construction begun. The mega-wall is a 22nd–24th century project — this phase lays the engineering and materials foundation.
  • Leakage within the prototype community estimated at 40–50% — substantially better than the 90% baseline as load-bearing technologies come online, but still far from civilizational deployment quality.
  • First public leakage report issued. The 0.01% aspiration formally adopted as the civilizational standard. Edge cases from the prototype community systematically catalogued.

Population: Prototype community of thousands. Leakage: ~90% → ~40–50% as load-bearing technologies cross initial thresholds.

2

Phase 2: Planetary Consolidation (2150 – 2350)

The full five-ring civilization reaches planetary scale and begins confronting the realities of long-duration operation.

  • Full planetary population across all rings reaches billions. Layer distribution stabilises across large populations.
  • Orbital habitats and first permanent off-world outposts established using VMSS principles.
  • Lifespan extension reaches 250–300+ years practical for most citizens.
  • Wall construction phases underway — prototype sections of the full 15km-above / 5km-below / 100m-thick boundary infrastructure completed and tested.
  • VMSS becomes the dominant governance model for new human settlements beyond Earth.
  • Early interstellar probe missions launched with backup vessel technology aboard.
  • System leakage estimated at approximately 25% by 2150 — formative infrastructure operational but incomplete. Edge cases from generations of operation systematically catalogued and addressed through charter amendment process.

Population: 1–5+ billion across the rings and beyond. Leakage: ~25% by 2150.

3

Phase 3: Infrastructure Hardening (2350 – 2500)

Two centuries of systematic construction and leakage reduction. The civilization transitions from prototype to proven architecture.

  • Full wall network construction across all five ring boundaries — the largest continuous engineering project in human history.
  • Sub-surface 5km wall foundations proven to eliminate tunnelling approaches. Above-stratosphere 15km height confirmed impervious to civilian aviation.
  • Backup vessel technology matures — drift risk declining from 2% toward sub-0.5% through successive generations of reconstruction science.
  • Enforcement drone coverage expands toward full saturation within Main Layer and above.
  • AI governance systems accumulate two centuries of edge case data — classification accuracy reaches new benchmarks.
  • The lower layer private economies mature. -3 sub-stratification into voluntary and punitive communities is well-established. Private justice infrastructure operates reliably across punitive layers.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~8% (2350) → ~4% (2400) → ~2% (2450) → ~1.5% (2500).
  • First Dyson swarm feasibility studies completed. Energy planning horizon set at 2600 for first partial deployment.

Leakage target by end of phase: ~1.5%

4

Phase 4: Systemic Refinement (2500 – 2600)

The civilization reaches operational maturity. Leakage enters single-digit territory. The focus shifts from building to refining.

  • Full wall network complete and operational across all layer boundaries. Boundary breach now requires extraordinary organised effort detectable by seismic and sensor networks before completion.
  • Backup vessel drift approaches near-zero through mature reconstruction biology. Continuity guarantee becomes reliable at civilizational scale.
  • Enforcement network achieves near-complete coverage — apprehension failure becomes the exception rather than the rule.
  • First partial Dyson swarm segments operational by 2600, beginning the transition to energy abundance beyond planetary renewable capacity.
  • Charter amendments through this period reflect 350+ years of lived civilizational experience — the document matures from founding framework to refined operating constitution.
  • Cross-layer cultural identity is well-established. The informal layer names — the Freedom Layer, the Balanced Layer, the Lower Restrictions Layer — are common civilizational vocabulary.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~1.5% (2500) → ~1.2% (2550) → ~1% (2600).

Leakage target by end of phase: ~1%

5

Phase 5: The Forcefield Century (2600 – 2850)

Dyson-class energy enables a new class of boundary infrastructure. The civilization prepares for its final approach to mature operation.

  • Dyson swarm energy output reaches levels sufficient for sustained planetary forcefield maintenance — the threshold that makes the forcefield upgrade viable shifts from theoretical to engineering.
  • Forcefield prototype tested on a single wall section by 2750. Results validate integration architecture.
  • Partial forcefield integration across primary layer boundaries by 2800. Wall breach leakage drops sharply — the category that was previously the largest single leakage source becomes functionally negligible.
  • Full forcefield network operational by 2850. Physical mega-wall and energy barrier now operate simultaneously — the boundary infrastructure is layered, redundant, and impenetrable by any means available to individuals or organised groups.
  • AI governance maturity reaches levels where edge case classification accuracy approaches theoretical limits. The system has now processed millennia-equivalent decision data through simulation and centuries of real operation.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~1% (2650) → ~0.5% (2750) → ~0.3% (2800) → ~0.1% (2850). Parabolic acceleration begins.

Leakage target by end of phase: ~0.1% — the parabolic acceleration commences

6

Phase 6: Mature VMSS (2850 – 3000)

The final approach. Technologies converge. Leakage collapses parabolically. The civilization the founding accord envisioned becomes real.

  • Dyson-class energy abundance enables enforcement network densities previously impossible — coverage gaps that produced apprehension failures essentially disappear when energy constraints are lifted.
  • Backup vessel drift eliminated as a meaningful failure category. Reconstruction biology has reached theoretical maturity — the continuity guarantee is as close to absolute as physics permits.
  • Forcefield and physical wall operating in complete integration. The boundary infrastructure of the civilization has no known bypass mechanism available to any non-civilizational actor.
  • AI governance systems operating at maximum achievable accuracy after nearly a millennium of refinement. The judgment calls that produced failures in formative phases are now handled with precision that would have seemed impossible to the founding generation.
  • The four major technology convergences — forcefield, energy abundance, backup vessel perfection, AI maturity — arriving within the same 150-year window compound each other's effect. This is the parabolic spike. The last 0.09% of leakage is eliminated faster than the preceding 24.91%.
  • System leakage trajectory: ~0.1% (2850) → ~0.05% (2900) → ~0.02% (2950) → ~0.01% (3000).
  • The civilization of 3000 measures its consequence delivery at 0.01% leakage across all categories. What the founding accord promised in 2026, the mature civilization delivers in 3000 — not perfectly, but as close to perfectly as any human institution has ever come.

Leakage target: ~0.01% — mature VMSS achieved

The Leakage Gradient

The trajectory figures above are weighted averages across all five layers. The headline number masks a meaningful spread — one that widens as the civilization matures and upper layers approach near-zero while the terminal layer maintains a structural floor. At key milestones the layer gradient looks approximately as follows:

Year +1 Sanctuary Main Layer -1 -2 -3 Terminal
2150~15%~20%~30%~40%~55%
2350~4%~6%~10%~15%~30%
2550~0.8%~1.5%~3%~5%~12%
2850~0.01%~0.05%~0.2%~0.4%~1.2%
3000~0.0001%~0.005%~0.05%~0.2%~1%

The terminal layer's ~1% floor at civilizational maturity is structural rather than technological — the inevitable expression of a fully privatised environment operating without institutional coverage. The 0.01% weighted average is the honest aggregate of five layers with five different design philosophies, each producing the leakage native to what it is.

From a single signed accord in Boise, Idaho in 2026 to a civilization operating at 0.01% leakage by the year 3000 — this is not fantasy.
This is a 974-year project built on moral causality, advanced technology, and the conviction that consequence and consequence delivery can approach perfection together.
