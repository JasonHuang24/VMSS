Enforcement & Restoration Protocol

How The Five Rings prevents, responds to, and restores after harm

The enforcement and restoration system is the backbone of moral causality in The Five Rings. It is divided into two primary modes depending on the layer: **Pre-Intervention** in Heaven Layers (+1 and SADs) and **Post-Intervention** in Main (0) and Hell Layers (-1, -2). The terminal -3 layer has no enforcement or restoration.

Pre-Intervention (Heaven Layers Only)

In +1 Sanctuary and all Selective Ascension Domains, the system uses the Threshold Inhibition Protocol (TIP) to halt harmful acts before they complete. This is the highest level of protection — harm is literally impossible in Heaven.

How Pre-Intervention Works

  • Implants continuously monitor neural patterns, motor intent, and behavioral context.
  • When intent + imminent execution is detected (e.g., raising a weapon toward someone, initiating non-consensual restraint), the implant triggers targeted motor inhibition (paralysis of relevant muscles) and/or short-range sedation via nano-release.
  • Ambient drones (part of Heaven infrastructure) deploy non-lethal countermeasures: nets, foam, sonic disorientation, or sedative mist.
  • The act is stopped mid-motion (e.g., knife freezes inches from target, assault halts before contact).
  • Perpetrator is immediately subdued and reassigned downward (usually to -3 for capital attempts).
  • Victim experiences the attempt but no physical harm; optional neural therapy processes trauma.

Why Pre-Intervention in Heaven?

Heaven Layers are gated by high compliance metrics (e.g., zero infidelity, zero distortions). Residents here have proven sustained non-harm behavior. The system extends maximum protection to this group, creating an environment of absolute safety and trust — the "heaven" promise fulfilled.

Post-Intervention (Main Layer & Hell Layers -1/-2)

In Main (0) and Hell Layers (-1, -2), the system allows acts to complete if the perpetrator ignores warnings/failsafes. This preserves full free will in the proving ground and lower strata. Victims are restored, perpetrators are removed.

How Post-Intervention Works

  • Implants warn of rising intent (“Intent approaching threshold”). User can override failsafe.
  • If the act completes (e.g., murder, rape), full incident is captured via implant/AR recordings + AI reconstruction.
  • Murder victims: Backup vessel revives them in the same layer's medical bay (with 0.5–2% drift risk).
  • Rape/other non-lethal harm victims: Immediate automated therapy (neural recalibration, physical restoration via augmentation).
  • Perpetrator: Reassigned downward immediately (e.g., murder → -3, severe assault → -2 or -3).
  • Evidence: Full recordings + reconstruction are irrefutable proof — logged, public for major violations.

Example Scenarios

  • Main Layer Murder Attempt: Act completes → victim revived in Main medical bay → perpetrator to -3.
  • -1 Assault: Act completes → victim treated → perpetrator to -2 or -3 if escalates.
  • Heaven Attempt: Act halted mid-motion → perpetrator to -3 → no harm occurs.

Why the Split?

Heaven Layers are for proven non-harmful citizens — the system extends maximum protection. Main and lower layers are proving grounds — free will must be real, even if risky. Victims are always restored (revival/therapy), perpetrators always face consequence (descent). This balance preserves both innocence and agency.

Edge Cases

  • Failsafe Disabled: User opts out → act completes → post-intervention triggers.
  • Multiple Perpetrators: All involved reassigned based on intent/execution level.
  • Victim Refuses Revival: Rare, but possible — continuity respected.
  • Visitor Breach: Locked in current layer (no return to higher).

Enforcement is not vengeance. It is consequence. Pre in Heaven protects the proven; post in Main tests the rest. The system measures — and acts.
