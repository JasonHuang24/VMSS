0 Main

The Proving Ground • Post-Intervention Enforcement

The 0 Main is the civic center and default entry point of The Five Rings Civilization. It is the largest and most populous ring, and the place where all new citizens begin. Here, individuals test their moral compliance, build relationships, pursue careers, raise families, and attempt to ascend to the Heaven Layers (+1). It is the great proving ground where freedom is at its widest and consequences are at their most real.

Overview & Purpose

The Main Layer serves as the bridge between the insulated perfection of Heaven and the raw consequences of the lower layers. It is deliberately designed as a neutral, high-freedom environment where citizens can live with abundance while still facing meaningful moral tests. Most residents spend the majority of their lives here, using it as a stable base from which to explore, create, and ascend — or from which they may fall if they cross critical thresholds.

Unlike the Heaven Layers where pre-intervention physically prevents harm from completing, the Main Layer operates on post-intervention principles. Harmful acts can and do occur, but the system ensures victims are restored and perpetrators face permanent consequences. This balance preserves genuine free will while protecting the long-term stability of the society.

Key Characteristics

  • Economy & Abundance: Every resident receives $10,000/month unconditional Universal Basic Income plus up to $10,000/month Primary Job Subsidy for one qualifying job. This creates true post-scarcity conditions for basic needs while still allowing status and luxury to be earned through contribution.
  • Enforcement Model: Post-Intervention. Serious harm (murder, rape, severe assault) can complete if the perpetrator ignores warnings and disables failsafes. Victims of murder are revived via backup vessel in the Main Layer medical bay. Rape and non-lethal harm victims receive immediate automated therapy and physical restoration. Perpetrators are reassigned downward immediately after the act.
  • Freedom & Autonomy: Full personal, creative, relational, and philosophical freedom. Consensual activities (fighting sports, adult entertainment, substance use, speech) are unrestricted unless they cross harm thresholds. No preemptive censorship or moral policing of thoughts or private behavior.
  • Population & Demographics: By far the largest ring. Home to the majority of citizens, including newcomers from Earth, long-term residents who prefer the balance of freedom and safety, and those who have chosen not to pursue further ascension.
  • Daily Infrastructure: High-tech but familiar urban/suburban feel. Automated services for food, medical care, education, and transportation. Neural diving hubs, augmentation clinics, and public forums are common.

A Day in the Life of a Main Layer Resident

Let's follow a typical resident named Elena Ruiz, a 29-year-old former software developer who joined VMSS two years ago.

She wakes gently in her 650 sq ft apartment as the smart glass windows transition from opaque to soft dawn light. No alarm — the implant gently pulses a sunrise simulation behind her left ear. She checks her personal dashboard: UBI and job subsidy credited, STI score steady at 92 (green), no pending thresholds. Breakfast fabricates in 14 seconds. She eats on the balcony overlooking the bustling Main Ring cityscape, watching drones glide silently overhead.

At 8:30 AM she joins a neural dive interview for a Heaven Layer research co-op. In 20 minutes she experiences the codebase from the lead developer's perspective. They mutually agree and she signs the contract neurally. By 11:00 AM she completes her mandatory new-citizen orientation dive — experiencing simulated threshold breaches and Heaven Layer life in audience mode. The experience is informative, not frightening.

Lunch with new friends at a public courtyard café (free food, obviously). Conversation flows easily — STI halos are all green, creating effortless trust. Afternoon free time: she does a voluntary audience-mode neural dive into a consenting Heaven resident's afternoon, feeling the deep calm of perfect safety. Evening: dinner with the same group, then some creative neural art session. She goes to bed knowing she is safe, solvent, and sovereign over her choices.

If she ever crosses a threshold (e.g., repeated deception or harm), the system will measure it and impose immediate layer reassignment — no mercy, no appeal.

Enforcement in Practice

In the Main Layer, serious harm can occur if the perpetrator actively defeats or ignores the system's warnings and failsafes. The implant provides escalating alerts and offers a failsafe freeze, but the user can disable it. If the act completes, the full incident is recorded via implant/AR sensors from multiple perspectives. The victim is revived via backup vessel (with possible minor drift), and the perpetrator faces immediate layer reassignment (usually to -3 Terminal for murder or severe violence).

Edge Cases & Unique Aspects

  • Attempted Harm That Fails: If the perpetrator tries but is stopped by the victim or bystanders (not the system), it may still trigger reassignment if intent + execution threshold is met.
  • Voluntary Descent from Main or Above: Residents can voluntarily descend to lower layers temporarily (e.g., for research, observation, or curiosity), but risk permanent lock-in if they commit a breach while there. Visitors from lower layers cannot ascend to Main or Heaven — upward movement is only through compliance and ascension, not visits.
  • Children Born in Lower Layers: Automatically relocated to Main autoparenting facilities upon birth.
  • STI & Social Dynamics: High STI unlocks better social and professional opportunities. Low STI limits trust-based interactions and can make daily life harder.
  • Backup Vessel Drift: Rare personality or memory shifts upon revival add psychological realism and stakes even in the generally safe Main Layer.
  • Cross-Layer Relationships: Common but complicated. Communication is possible but sanitized. Families often have members in different rings.

The Main Layer is the crucible where the philosophy of The Five Rings is truly tested. It offers the greatest freedom and the clearest mirror of one's own character.
