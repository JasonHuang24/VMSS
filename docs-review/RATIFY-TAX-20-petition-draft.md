# RATIFY-TAX-20 — Top-Marginal Rate Recalibration

**Federal LP petition · DRAFT · UNMERGED · GATED — not enacted, not in the registry.**

> **Gate status: UNSAID.** This file drafts the petition only. It ships to `law-polling.html`
> as an enacted entry **only on founder ratification after the full Article XXV.VI gauntlet**,
> and only once the LP-070 treasury coverage ratio reads at or above its threshold from
> automation-side revenue. Until then the live schedule — **70 / 35 / 17 / 8** — stands
> untouched everywhere. Nothing in this draft modifies a rate table.

## Petition

Recalibrate the top-marginal rate schedule of Whitepaper §12.1 (the binding source; Charter
III.III restates it per RULING-TIER / LP-070) from the current cascade to a flatter one:

| Layer | Current top marginal | Proposed | Retention at the top |
|---|---|---|---|
| +1 Sanctuary & Main Layer | 70% | **20%** | 80¢ on the marginal dollar |
| -1 Noncompliance | 35% | **10%** | 90¢ |
| -2 Violent Offense | 17% | **5%** | 95¢ |
| -3 Terminal | 8% | **2.5%** | 97.5¢ |

The $10M/yr earned-income threshold and every Savings Circulation Mandate parameter are
unchanged. The cascade keeps the halving-per-descent shape.

## Premise — velocity and legibility

Two instruments, two functions (Charter III.III): the tax collects revenue; the Savings
Circulation Mandate prevents hoarding. The premise of this petition is that the second
instrument has matured to the point where the first no longer needs to run punitive at the top
to do anti-concentration work it was never the right tool for. With attribution (LP-069) closing
the second-residence, collateral, and barter seams, and the idle-commercial enforcement layer
(LP-070) closing the simulated-settlement seam, **idle wealth is recirculated as everyone's
dividend by the pulse, not by the income bracket.** A 70% top marginal, in that architecture,
taxes *flow* to suppress *stock* — the wrong lever. Dropping it to 20% lets high earners retain
80% of the marginal dollar (velocity: that capital either moves or the pulse takes it), and it
collapses the gap between the headline rate and what the system actually relies on (legibility:
the SCM is named as the anti-concentration instrument, in the open, rather than quietly
back-stopping a bracket no one defends on revenue grounds).

## Disclosed costs — named, not buried (§28.0 convention)

1. **The elite liquid ceiling rises.** At a 20% top marginal, the sustainable liquid position of
   a top earner rises toward a disclosed **~$2B-equivalent** ceiling before the pulse equilibrium
   and the (unchanged) descent-liquidation schedule bound it. This is a real increase in visible
   elite wealth, accepted as the price of velocity — the aspirational economy Charter III.III
   already defends, run hotter. The SCM still deflates any static hoard above the district
   equilibrium; what rises is the flow-through, not a protected stock.
2. **Incidence shifts onto triggered-district savers.** Cutting the top bracket moves the
   civilization's revenue weight toward the SCM pulse, which lands on every saver in an
   *activated* district — not only the top earners the old bracket targeted. A middle saver in a
   hot district carries relatively more of the load than before. Disclosed plainly: the petition
   trades a progressive-on-flow instrument for a proportional-on-idle-stock one, and the
   distributional consequence rides along.

## The gate — treasury coverage (RULING-POCKET / LP-070)

The rates fall **only as automation demonstrably carries the institutional load the tax now
carries.** Per LP-070's Main-treasury architecture, recalibration conditions on the treasury's
coverage ratio holding **≥ 120% over a trailing 36-month window, with no single month below 100%**, from automation-side revenue. This petition is inert
until that gate reads green: it is not a standing promise to cut, it is a pre-drafted cut that
arms when automation revenue covers the treasury's enumerated obligations without the top-bracket
contribution. If the ratio later slips below threshold, the same ladder repeals it.

## Classification

Federal, Article XXV.VI. Per RULING-TIER (recorded in LP-070's dual-key note), the top-marginal
figures are federal-law-tier; recalibrating them is federal jurisdiction and does not reach the
Charter amendment process or the founding core. The Supreme Court 6/10 gate confirms the boundary

## What this petition does NOT do

- It does not touch the $10M/yr threshold, the bracket structure below it, or any SCM parameter.
- It does not alter currency siloing, descent liquidation, or the reproduction escalation.
- It does not modify the live 70/35/17/8 schedule anywhere on the site — that changes only on
  ratification, at which point §12.1 is amended and the III.III restatement marker is updated to
  match.
