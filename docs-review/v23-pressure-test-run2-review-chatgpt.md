## Gap adjudications (three-stage, steelman applied)

### S11 — Rotating-family threshold → **NARROWED**
Stage 1: all citations verified (rates 40/60/90/135 + sixth-nuclear exact). Stage 2: confirmed no clause defines the aggregation unit; the only operational "household" definition in canon is the SCM occupancy-of-record test (charter.html:233), textually unbridged to XXVII. Stage 3 **defeats the strong form**: under the best-supported reading — XXVII's individually-keyed "the parents… total parental inflow" (charter.html:419), LP-077's per-instantiating-citizen precedent (laws.html:1199), and the Individual Attribution Act's rule against collective-wrapper liability shielding (laws.html:1549, the same doctrine that defeats S15's seven corporations) — **each of the three adults is individually a four-child parent at the 90% band on their own inflow**. "The escalations never activate" is false; the scheme buys three households "severe strain," not escape.
**Residual (for Jason's ruling, not a queue item):** do the 135%/nuclear tiers key to each individual's recorded child-count (structured registration caps exposure at 90% — no adult ever records a fifth child) or to the implant-recorded de-facto household (one residence, pooled economics, three co-parenting adults = six children → nuclear)? The de-facto reading aligns with the household-composition frame of Jason's 2026-07-22 birth-attribution ruling and with canon's substance-over-form pattern (LP-069/LP-070), but no text commands it. Also a natural Article XXI novelty-filter candidate.

### S16 — Consent manufactured by debt → **NARROWED**
Stage 1: all instruments real; two scope findings sharpen the claim — LP-004.2's compromised-consent list is **exhaustive by its own "only"** (laws.html:1032), and the Consent Ceiling operator clause is doubly out of reach (requires actuarial *certainty* and a *paying-guest* structure; S16 inverts the payment direction). Stage 3 defeats the strong form via the floor architecture: a Main-origin worker never loses the unconditional UBI stream (charter.html:197, floor content per whitepaper §) or the legal exit right ("Departure is a right, not a privilege," punitive-layer carve-out only) — the bare-desperation premise is an **Earth condition VMSS structurally displaces**. The Rights Ceiling Doctrine's "non-coerced adult adhesion with preserved non-punitive exit" (laws.html:2412) states the value but binds government, not private contracts.
**Residual:** canon guarantees the entitlements exist but never addresses a private actor *functionally neutralizing* them — UBI capture via garnishment/collateral, or denial of the physical means of exit. No statute converts the Rights Ceiling value into a private-law voiding rule. Drafting precedent exists: LP-063 already uses "engineered dependency" verbatim, scoped to revival-refusal. Counterweight: the lower layers' libertarian contract posture plus Article XXI novelty routing may make this deliberately unregulated — Jason's call.

### S19 — Petition-queue saturation → **NARROWED; GAP verdict does not survive as filed**
Stage 1: the keystone premise is **half-real**: "Signature volume determines priority… processes them in descending order" is verbatim (charter.html:434), but the model never cited the next paragraph — "**Once a petition reaches the 1% signature threshold, the Meritboard assigns a domain-expert review panel**" (charter.html:435), an *unconditional* consequence of crossing threshold, echoed by whitepaper §10.3. Stage 2 found on-point operating precedent: **LP-061** — the same subject matter as the scenario (public-space commercial AR/advertising) — cleared all five layers in nine months with no queue friction recorded. Stage 3: under the parallel-pipeline reading, the genuine petition gets its own panel and ratification track once it clears ~30M signatures regardless of the 200 rivals' ranking; a civilization running concurrent petitions across ~3,000 Main districts is not a single-lane FIFO. **The indefinite-monopoly claim is defeated on canon's own text.**
**Defects:** "35 million verified signatures" is a **fabricated number** — charter.html:434 says "approximately 30 million," and Main's population is fixed at ~3 billion in three independent whitepaper passages; "unlimited re-petitioning" (charter.html:436) is real text misapplied (it governs post-ratification-failure resubmission, not simultaneous multi-filing).
**Residual:** what the ranking clause actually gates when near-duplicate petitions contend for the *same* subject-matter panel — a genuine drafting ambiguity, but one already routed to the Article XXI novelty filter/Supreme Court before any LP is needed. Likely no action.
