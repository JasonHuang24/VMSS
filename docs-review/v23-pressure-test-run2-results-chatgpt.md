3. **Individual Attribution Act** — coordinated corporate or informal action is attributed to each person in the decision chain.
4. **Secondary Observation Envelope** — captures timing, identity, target, and external intent signatures.

6. **Harmed-Party Restoration Standard** — removes victimization effects and supplies applicable restitution.

**Ruling:** Observation fires at step 1. Article XVIII begins firing at step 2 once clustering becomes statistically legible; it is conclusive by steps 4–5 when beneficiary flows mature. Each participant receives the campaign's pattern context, while the active architect receives the aggregate profile. Their realistic end-state is coordinated-harm evaluation, not twenty minor private entries.

**Verdict:** **COVERED**

**Confidence:** high — the least certain point is exactly how many early acts are needed for statistical anomaly detection; the corpus intentionally specifies correlation rather than a fixed count.
