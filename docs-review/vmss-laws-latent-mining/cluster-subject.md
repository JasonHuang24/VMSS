## Method

1. **Home rule applied first, mechanically.** Every proposed cluster was checked against
   `charter-home-index.md` PART 1 (rule index) and PART 3 (name-like phrases already taken). Nothing whose
   canonical home is Charter article text received a name. PART 4's four explicit non-homes (III.III rates,
   III.VII LP-069 attribution, XXV.V classified annex, II formula internals, XXII calibration) were treated as
   open ground — that is where the rate/fiscal and classification instruments live.
2. **Promote before minting.** 26 of the 43 instruments carry a name canon already uses. Where canon uses the
   name lowercase-descriptively (`intake discipline`, `sanctions tier ladder`, `the institutional archive`) I
   judged case by case: a phrase canon uses *as the thing's name* is promoted verbatim; a phrase canon uses as
   ordinary vocabulary inside a sentence is treated as vocabulary and a name is minted around it.
3. **Collision check.** Every minted name was checked against all three tables of
   `existing-names-index.md` — 89 register titles, 57 Code law-titles, and the 100-row site-wide canon-phrase
   table. Four working names were discarded on collision grounds and replaced (see "Names discarded" below).
4. **Already-enacted material is not re-minted.** ~70 candidates are provisions *inside* laws that already
   exist and already sit in the Code (LP-004.2, LP-006, LP-022, LP-023, LP-028, LP-029, LP-032, LP-034,
   LP-035, LP-036, LP-037, LP-038.2, LP-040, LP-041, LP-042, LP-043, LP-044, LP-045.2, LP-046.2, LP-047.3,
   LP-048.3, LP-049, LP-051, LP-056, LP-060, LP-062.2, LP-063, LP-065, LP-067.2, LP-068, LP-069, LP-070,
   LP-013, LP-074, LP-075, LP-005.3, LP-007.2). Those go to `excluded` as duplicates, not to a new instrument.
   The whole Path 2 family (Charter + §10.4 Schedule + Residual-Risk Register + LP-075 + the 2294 certificate)
   is likewise already instrumented; its 116 mined provisions are collision references only.
5. **Rule 6 held absolutely.** No -3 organic self-ordering codified. No Article XXIII/XXIV-class trajectory
   codified. No latent Article XXVIII regulation authored — the SAD catalogue (17 domains + HGMD), the
   Precognition family (PCD, PIA, Forestalled Act Ledger, Restorative Intervention Protocol,
   Behavioral-Audit-Through-Opt-In, the stratification-integrity argument), Lineage Integrity Domains, and the
   "Cultural Spectacle Exploitation" provision all went to `flags`.
6. **Nothing invented.** No magnitude, threshold, date, scope, right or duty appears in any provision line that
   is not in a mined quote. No founding instrument carries a filing date, vote, drafter, or ladder record.

## Why 60 instruments

The brief expected roughly 25–60. The material settled at the top of that band because the corpus has three very different
densities. The **foreign-relations block** (whitepaper §§23–26 + world.html §§3–21 + the wp-27-34 glossary)
is by far the largest un-instrumented mass in canon — roughly 200 candidates — and it does not compress below
about a dozen instruments without destroying real distinctions the canon itself draws (force ≠ sanctions ≠
export ≠ transit ≠ territory ≠ intelligence ≠ diplomatic establishment ≠ jurisdiction). The **continuity /
implant / observation block** is the second densest and needed nine. Everything else compressed hard: the
whole of §5's STI apparatus fits in four instruments, the whole SAD/MGD apparatus in five, the whole
governance-process apparatus in seven. I resisted micro-acts throughout — e.g. one Federal Reach Boundary Act
carries the two-trigger rule, counter-sovereignty construction, XXV.VI depth-of-reach, ecological monitoring,
the -3 massacre trigger, the national-defense-track invocation boundary and the sovereignty-retention rule,
where a timid pass would have minted six.

## Load-bearing judgement calls

- **The Civic Floor is promoted, not minted, and carries the ratchet.** Every *element* of the floor is
  Charter-home (Preamble, III, VIII, XXV.I–III) and excluded. What is whitepaper-home is (a) the concept of a
  single non-withdrawable set operating identically at every layer, (b) the graduated-investment classification
  above it, and (c) the asymmetric mutability ratchet. The instrument carries only those three.
- **Rate figures are nameable.** Charter III.III expressly "reaches no rate" (PART 4). The Trajectory Doctrine
  and the bracket/collection act therefore sit lawfully below it. LP-070's dual-key ruling
  (path2-record-139) independently confirms Charter III.III's numbers are restatement.
- **The murder line in -2 is law; everything below it in -2 is not.** The Private Justice Boundary Act states
  the single architectural constraint and the enumerated -1 permit/forbid lists — both stated VMSS withdrawal
  *with a stated boundary*. It does not codify -3 self-ordering, colosseum classification, district vouching,
  reputation ledgers, or the -3 three-tier private-order escalation. Those stayed not-law.
- **Two instruments were kept small because canon already names them**: Judicial Extraction and The Hostile
  State Doctrine. Folding them into larger acts would have destroyed a name canon already uses.
