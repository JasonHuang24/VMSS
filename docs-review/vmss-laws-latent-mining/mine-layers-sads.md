## Method notes

- Every candidate below carries a VERBATIM quote (HTML tags stripped only; `&mdash;`/`&rsquo;`/`&sect;`
  entities rendered to their characters; nothing else altered). Hedges preserved exactly.
- Charter cross-check performed for each: `also_in_charter` records the article where the rule's canonical
  home sits, or `""` where none was found.
- Per the tier test, **lawmaking machinery** (Art. XI / XXI / XXII / XXV.VI / XXVIII) and **layer-membership
  criteria** (STI thresholds, 85-point floor, phasing criteria, descent triggers, permanence) are
  Charter-tier by definition and are recorded as `charter-home-excluded`.
- **-3 organic self-ordering is marked `not-law` throughout**, per canon's own insistence. That includes the
  Wild Enterprise / Legality-by-Local-Endorsement frame, the Colosseum Classification (already canon-named,
  but customary -3 law), Voluntary District Governance, and the Practical Justice three-tier escalation.
- `Article XXVIII` regulatory mechanics restated in each of the five dossiers' "Regulatory Governance"
  bullets are one excluded family, recorded once with all five anchors.
