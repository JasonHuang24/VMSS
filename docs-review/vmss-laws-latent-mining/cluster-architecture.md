# LATENT-CORPUS CODIFICATION SWEEP — CLUSTER ARCHITECTURE

Branch `feat/vmss-laws-v22.7.0`. Inputs: `charter-home-index.md` (veto list, read in full),
`existing-names-index.md` (collision index, read in full), all 14 `mine-*.md` surface reports,
and the inlined candidate set.

## 1. METHOD

**Lens.** Clustered by VMSS architectural function — the load-bearing systems the civilization
actually runs on — not by source page and not by deontic type. Six spines organise the set:

1. The **ledger / implant enforcement spine** (observation, admissibility, correction, classification)
2. The **currency and fiscal silo** (central bank, collection, treasury, evasion)
3. The **continuity stack** (revival, finality, consent, bailout, archive)
4. The **cross-layer membrane** (status, transit, boundary, private justice, exit)
5. The **external boundary** (defence, force, alliance, sanctions, export, border, citizenship)
6. The **domain-charter system** (SADs, MGDs, civic floor, federal reach)

**Home rule applied first.** Every cluster was checked against `charter-home-index.md` PART 1
before naming. Where a rule's canonical home is Charter article text it was excluded outright,
even where the whitepaper states it more fully. Currency siloing, the upward-conversion
prohibition, the SCM rates and triggers, the VPR retention bands, the UBI/PJS schedules, the
three-axis framework, the two-trigger federal floor's *triggers*, the four gauntlet gates, the
kill switch and nanobot plumes — all Charter-home, all excluded. The nameable residue in each
of those families is the un-homed surround: the central bank's constitution, the settlement-layer
collection architecture, the *boundary* rule on the two triggers, and so on.

**Promote before minting.** 23 instruments carry names canon already supplies. Convention used:
`origin: promoted` where the canon phrase is the instrument's own self-name, with at most a
leading "The" and a single instrument-class suffix (Act / Doctrine / Standard / Specification)
appended — e.g. canon "civic floor" → *The Civic Floor Act*. Anything further from the canon
phrase is `minted` and was collision-checked against all 165 names in `master.txt`, the 89
law-polling titles, the 57 `laws.html` code-entry titles, and the Charter's PART 3 reserved-phrase
list.

**Number.** 56 instruments. The material supported far more micro-acts (a first pass produced 69);
eleven were merged where the provisions shared a single load-bearing function — intake discipline
and signal integrity into the ledger act, victim restoration into the enforcement chain, analog
redundancy into the redundant-envelope pattern, officeholder accountability into governance
restraint, carceral limits into private justice, layer invariance into consequence classification,
treasury discipline into monetary sovereignty, leakage accounting into transparency, autoparenting
capacity into clean-record, revival finality into the continuity guarantee, and the orphan rights
into the rights ceiling. 56 sits inside the 25–60 band and each instrument carries between two
and twenty-one traceable provisions.

## 2. WHAT WAS NOT CODIFIED

- **Charter-home rules.** The home rule is absolute. Naming these federally would recreate the
  exact tier confusion the v22.7.0 restructure just cured.
- **Already-enacted named instruments.** LP-004.2, LP-005.3, LP-006, LP-007.2, LP-013, LP-022,
  LP-023, LP-028, LP-029, LP-032, LP-034, LP-035, LP-036, LP-037, LP-038.2, LP-040, LP-041,
  LP-042, LP-043, LP-044, LP-045.2, LP-046.2, LP-047.3, LP-048.3, LP-049, LP-051, LP-056, LP-060,
  LP-062.2, LP-063, LP-064, LP-065, LP-067.2, LP-068, LP-069, LP-070, LP-074, LP-075 — these are
  not latent. They are collision references.
- **The entire Path 2 family.** Path 2 Charter, its §10.4 Schedule, the Residual-Risk Register,
  LP-075 and the 2294 certification are already instrumented end to end. 130 of 138 path2 records
  are collision references; the residue is flagged, not named.
- **Failed and superseded filings.** LP-005.2 (superseded), LP-058, LP-059, LP-062, LP-066, and the
  Distributed Citizenship amendment never took force. A failed vote is a boundary marker, not law.
- **-3 organic self-ordering.** Reputation ledgers, district vouching, private supermax,
  colosseum customary classification, market associations. Canon insists this is not law and it
  is never codified here. Only the *federal boundary* around it is named.
- **Aspirations.** Article XXIII/XXIV-class leakage trajectories, medical completeness, Dyson-class
  energy, the Five Planet Federation horizon, ASI-maturity projections, lifestyle and cultural text.
- **Latent regulatory-tier items.** Article XXVIII regulations are petitioned by definition. The SAD
  catalogue, every per-domain gating schedule, the Precognition Covenant Domain and its whole
  instrument stack (PIA, Restorative Intervention Protocol, Forestalled Act Ledger,
  Behavioral-Audit-Through-Opt-In, the stratification-integrity argument), the Lineage Integrity
  Domains, and the "Cultural Spectacle Exploitation" provision all go to flags. Authoring an
  unpetitioned regulation would contradict the mechanism.
- **Simulation-narrative-only rules.** The high-consequence certification standards (non-sentience
  criteria, inhabitant-status disclosure, non-recording clause, standing observation protocol,
  non-zero-exit condition) are quoted operative text whose only home is a v16.5 Doctrine-Snapshot
  card. Flagged for corroboration rather than codified. The one exception is the doctrinal-
  acknowledgment form, which `why-vmss.html` independently corroborates.

## 4. CROSS-INSTRUMENT TENSIONS RECORDED (no candidate reassignment implied)

- **Implant mandate vs preserved opt-in.** whitepaper §4.2 line 438 "Implants mandatory for all
  residents" (+1) against §4.4 line 455 listing "preserved implant opt-in" as civic-floor content.
  Both verbatim. Not adjudicated.
- **Territorial-doctrine charter citation.** `world.html` lines 1114/1126 and 1034 attribute the
  conquest and expansion prohibitions to "the founding charter"; `charter.html` contains no such
  article. The prohibition's only textual home is world-tier.
- **Refugee category.** `world.html` §6 states operative asylum-channel rules; §14 line 1016 states
  "The civilization does not operate a separate refugee or asylum category."
- **Transit-right scope.** Glossary §34 grants persons a transit right against incidental
  jurisdiction; §26.5 body text states "No blanket transit rights exist" for state vessels.
  Different subjects, same name.
- **Longevity export.** `world.html` §8 states an absolute prohibition on the longevity stack
  leaving VMSS borders; the §16 Tier 0 enumeration does not list it.
- **Foreign-born child UBI.** Same paragraph places UBI eligibility both "from birth by parentage"
  and at implant installation.
- **Popular signal correction threshold.** "a sufficient volume of citizens" states no numeric
  threshold anywhere in canon.
