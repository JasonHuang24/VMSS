## Notes for the clustering stage

1. **Do not mint the currency-siloing prohibition.** It reads like the archetypal latent founding act, but Article III.IV (charter.html:200–201) carries it verbatim. Charter home → excluded.
2. **Three genuine founding-act families emerge on this surface:**
   - *Monetary institution* — wp-09-12-59 + wp-09-12-60 (central bank constitution + its negative competence) + wp-09-12-55 (external inconvertibility). These three cohere as one instrument: the silo wall and the authority that maintains it.
   - *Fiscal machinery below the rate schedule* — wp-09-12-44 (layer-administered brackets), wp-09-12-46 (settlement-layer collection), wp-09-12-47 (cash-analog leakage do-nothing), wp-09-12-48 (**The Trajectory Doctrine**, already named — promote).
   - *Anti-smuggling / anti-sabotage* — wp-09-12-33 (experimental relaxation cannot bypass the amendment tier) and wp-09-12-81 (laundering & tax evasion as system sabotage). Different domains, same architectural instinct; wp-09-12-81 is load-bearing for LP-070's wash-clearing rule.
   - Standalone: wp-09-12-13 (federal genetic-level ecological monitoring reaching -3).
3. **Every LP-numbered rule on this surface is already enacted law** (LP-004.2, LP-006, LP-041, LP-044, LP-046.2, LP-064, LP-065, LP-069, LP-070, LP-074, LP-075). They are recorded as `schedule-under-authority` for completeness with their verbatim law-polling titles, marked DO NOT MINT.
4. **One regulatory-flag:** wp-09-12-18, the -3 Article XXVIII advisory regulation "enacted within a month at 93% ratification" (whitepaper:783), which has no law-polling register entry. Flag only — Article XXVIII regulations are petitioned by definition and are not authorable.
5. **Hedges preserved verbatim** in the quotes above and must survive downstream: "advisory only", "approximately", "in principle", "tends to be" (none on this surface but present at whitepaper:382 in the adjacent Charter text), "prices … rather than pretending to close", "founder-calibratable", "a sustained window", "rules this a do-nothing", "Named rather than denied".
6. **Not carried on this surface but present in the Charter** (so *not* a whitepaper gap, just noting for the crosswalk): "There is no minimum wage in VMSS" (charter.html:190) has no whitepaper §11 counterpart. If the Code wants that rule, its home is Article III.II.
7. **Two do-nothing tripwires** (wp-09-12-77, wp-09-12-78) carry explicit numeric reopening conditions and cite the §28.0 redundant-envelope naming convention. They are conditions on future lawmaking, not rules of conduct.
